### Voice Interaction Demo (90 seconds)
**Visual**: Enter the interrogation screen
**Narration**: 
> "Instead of typing questions, players can speak naturally to the mystical orb. Watch as we investigate Lord Blackwood's murder using only our voice."

**Live Demo**:
1. **Click voice mode** - Show the microphone interface
2. **Ask first question**: "Who had access to the wine?"

3. **Ask second question**: "What about the timing of the poison?"
   - Demonstrate contextual AI responses
   - Show how Gemini maintains case consistency

   - Show the final clue being revealed

**Visual**: Split screen showing code/architecture
**Narration**:

- Show the Gemini API integration generating responses
- Demonstrate ElevenLabs voice synthesis in real-time
